/********************************3d Bowling ******************************/
Files that are contained in the directory to be loaded by the program:

cubo.wrl
pinos.wrl
fita.wrl
bola.wrl
cenario.wrl

How to operate the program
Keys:
'q' to leave
'e' disables lighting
'w' enable lighting
'r' resets the game
'z', 'x', 'c', 's' rotate the camera 
'1' restores the camera's initial position;

Mouse:
Single click with the left button launches the ball in the direction of the mouse.


Known bugs:
- The calculation of the collisions is not very precise, so for a few times pins that are not in the trajectory of the ball are knocked over.
